Method & Sources

Our approach is built on three core principles that ensure the quality and reliability of our analysis:

  • Critical Use of Sources: We evaluate each source for credibility, bias, and methodological rigor. Only peer-reviewed research, official statistics, and verified data are included in our studies.
  • Transparency: Every claim is backed by clearly cited sources. Our methodology is open and documented, allowing anyone to verify our findings and understand our analytical process.
  • Reproducibility: We provide all necessary information for others to reproduce our analysis. Data sources, statistical methods, and analytical frameworks are fully documented.
